Property Tax Calculator

Property Tax = (Annual Value x Tax Rate)


Annual Value(AV)

1. Unit area value per square metre (UAVpsm) is the value of each square metre of the property, based on its location and use.
2. Unit area of property (UAP) is the total area of the property in square metres.
3. Age factor (AF) is a multiplier that reflects the age of the property.
4. Use factor (UC) is a multiplier that reflects the use of the property.
5. Structure factor (SF) is a multiplier that reflects the quality of construction of the property.
6. Occupancy factor (OF) is a multiplier that reflects the occupancy of the property.
7. Rate of tax is the percentage of the annual value that is payable as property tax.

AV = UAVpsm x UAP x UC x AF x SF x OF

Property Tax = AV x Tax Rate
